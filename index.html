Event Line‑up

One‑Round events [1‑R] • Two‑Round events [2‑R] • Categories: JSC

English Olympiad (J, S, C) [1‑R] Instant Story Writing (J, S, C) [1‑R] Word Game (J, S, C) [1‑R] Extempore Speech (J, S, C) [2‑R] Turn Coat (S, C) [2‑R] Quizmania (J, S, C) [1‑R] Outline Movies (J, S, C) [1‑R] Spell Master (J, S, C) [2‑R] Submissions: Wall Mag / Scrapbook / Sketch / Article / Photography (J, S, C) Mega / Team Quiz (S, C) [2‑R] Multimedia + Animation (S, C) [1‑R] Sports Quiz (J, S, C) [1‑R] Poem Recitation (J, S, C) [2‑R] Blog (J, S) [1‑R] Graffiti (J, S, C) [1‑R] Signature Event: 404 Not Found (J, S, C)
English Olympiad
Solo • 25 mins • Grammar, literature, vocab • [1‑R]
A classic written test covering multiple‑choice, one‑word and short descriptive questions to assess overall command of English across Junior, Senior and College groups.
Instant Story Writing
Solo • Prompt‑based • 25 mins • [1‑R]
Unleash the storyteller within. Receive a prompt and craft a compelling short story within the time limit.
Word Game
Solo • Jumbled Words + Crossword • 25 mins • [1‑R]
Test vocabulary and pattern recognition with letter jumbles and a themed crossword.
Extempore Speech
Solo • Topic given 3–4 mins prior • [2‑R]
Speak on a surprise topic with minimal prep. Top 6 per group advance to a final round.
Turn Coat
Solo • For/Against same topic • [2‑R] (S, C)
Argue both sides of a motion with timed flips. Preliminary: 1.5+1.5 mins; Final: 2+2 mins.
Quizmania
Solo quiz • 25 mins • [1‑R]
From Literature to Sherlock, Potterhead, Marvel/DC and Sports—pick your passion and ace the paper.
Outline Movies
Solo • 25 mins • [1‑R]
Summarize a movie’s arc with clarity and style—no spoilers needed, just great storytelling.
Spell Master
Written + Verbal • [2‑R]
Round 1: 30‑min written spell test. Round 2: on‑stage verbal for the title of master speller.
Submissions
Wall Magazine • Scrapbook • Sketch • Photography • Article
Wall Magazine (Team 2–3): Up to 6×4 ft on cork; submit Day‑1 8:30–10:30; present to judges. Respect community guidelines.
Scrapbook (Solo): Personal themed book with research, visuals and design; submit on campus.
Sketch (Solo): Any concept on white cartridge paper; submit in time; no vulgar content.
Photography (Solo – DSLR/Mobile): Local culture & life; minor edits allowed; up to 5 photos; JPEG/PNG; ≤10 MB each.
Article (Solo): Original, insightful piece on campus submission.
Mega / Team Quiz
Teams of 3–5 • Written preliminary + Stage buzzer • 6k prize pool
Top six teams reach a buzzer final; open team composition across institutions. Prizes: 3k / 2k / 1k.
Multimedia + Animation Presentation
Teams of 2 • 5‑min presentation • Single round
Create and deliver a polished multimedia pitch. Bring your own laptop/device. Content must respect guidelines.
Sports Quiz
Solo written • [1‑R]
All things sport—from classics to current highlights.
Poem Recitation
Solo performance • [2‑R]
Choose any poem; top 6 per group progress to the final performance.
Blog
Solo writing • 25 mins • [1‑R] (J,S)
Write like a digital storyteller: clarity, voice and audience engagement matter.
Graffiti
Solo art • 1 hour • [1‑R]
Turn a blank board into bold expression—impactful, original, unmistakably you.
Signature Event: 404 Not Found
Teams of 2 • Race‑style puzzle hunt • 6k prize pool
A high‑energy chase through riddles, tasks and hidden clues. Fastest team to the destination wins. Prizes: 3k / 2k / 1k.
